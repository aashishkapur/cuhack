A platform to connect high school tutors to students who need tutoring

Learning and earning at the tap of a button. High school students can now share their skills and help younger students through tutoring them in subjects that benefit them.



  • I want to be tutored

  • I want to become a tutor
Live Video Tutoring
Tutors and Tutees can choose to have an online live tutoring session or an in person tutoring session.
Safe Payments
All payments are processed through the platform, making paying for tutors safer and easier.
Instantly Find Tutors or Tutees
Find an experienced high school tutor or a student who needs a tutor within seconds.
Smart Matching
Our platform matches tutors and tutees based on their personality and preferred teaching and learning styles.
